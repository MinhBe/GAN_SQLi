10 GE Connectivity
High speed connectivity is essential for network security 
segmentation at the core of data networks. The FortiGate 
2000E provides high 10 GE port densities, simplifying network 
designs without relying on additional devices to bridge 
desired connectivity.
NP Direct
By removing the Internal Switch Fabric, the NP Direct 
architecture provides direct access to the SPU-NP for the 
lowest latency forwarding. NGFW deployments require some 
attention to network design to ensure optimal use of this 
technology.
