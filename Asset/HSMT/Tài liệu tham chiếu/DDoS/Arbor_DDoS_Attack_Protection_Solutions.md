## Product Portfolio Overview

Arbor offers a comprehensive portfolio of fully integrated, in-cloud and on-premise DDoS protection products and services, all backed by continuous global threat intelligence from ATLAS and ASERT.

---

## Arbor APS (Availability Protection System)

| Specification | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deployment** | Always on, in-line detection and mitigation |
| **Mitigation Range** | Sub 100 Mbps to 40 Gbps |
| **Platforms** | Available as appliance or virtual platform |
| **Optional Service** | Managed service available |
| **Attack Types Stopped** | Inbound and outbound DDoS attacks, malware, C2 communication |
| **Cloud Integration** | Cloud Signaling provides intelligent integration with Arbor Cloud |

---

## Arbor SP + Threat Management System (TMS)

| Specification | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Arbor SP** | Pervasive network visibility and DDoS attack detection (in as little as 1 second) using NetFlow, BGP, and SNMP data |
| **Arbor TMS** | Out-of-path, stateless, surgical mitigation of DDoS attacks |
| **TMS Mitigation Capacity** | Up to 400 Gbps per appliance |
| **Platform Options** | 6U chassis, 2U appliances, Cisco ASR 9000 router embedded |
| **Deployment** | Shared scrubbing center or embedded in Cisco ASR9K router |
| **Auto-Reroute** | Can automatically re-route attack traffic to TMS upon detection |

## Key Features and Benefits

- **Proven and Trusted:** 17+ years as undisputed leader in DDoS attack protection
- **Layered Protection:** Hybrid approach addressing volumetric, TCP state exhaustion, and application-layer attack vectors
- **Flexible Deployment:** On-premise appliances, virtual machines, network-embedded (Cisco ASR 9000), cloud services
- **Intelligent Automation:** Automated detection, mitigation, and cloud signaling
- **Global Intelligence:** Continuous threat intelligence from ATLAS/ASERT

---

## Attack Types Covered

1. **Volumetric Attacks** - High-volume flood attacks targeting internet connectivity (mitigated in cloud)
2. **TCP State Exhaustion Attacks** - State-exhaustion attacks (mitigated on-premise)
3. **Application-Layer Attacks** - Targeted application attacks (mitigated on-premise)
