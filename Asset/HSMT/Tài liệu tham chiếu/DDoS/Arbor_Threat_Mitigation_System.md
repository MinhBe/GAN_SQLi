## Product Overview

The Arbor Threat Mitigation System (TMS) is the acknowledged leader in DDoS protection. Working in conjunction with Arbor Sightline (which provides visibility and threat detection), TMS adapts to attacks as they change, surgically removing DDoS attack traffic from your network without disrupting key network services.

## Key Features

- **Advanced Detection and Mitigation:** Surgically removes up to 50 Tbps of DDoS attack traffic while enabling legitimate traffic flow
- **Flexible and Scalable Solution:** Choice of platforms and configurations to meet diverse deployment needs
- **Virtualized Attack Mitigation:** Entire Sightline & TMS deployment can be 100% virtualized (SDN/NFV)
- **DDoS Service Offering:** Service Providers can extend protection to customers and monetize the investment

## Deployment

- **Physical Appliances:** Available in multiple mitigation platforms and capacities
- **Virtual Appliances:** 100% virtualizable for SDN/NFV environments
- **Integration:** Works with Arbor Sightline for visibility and threat detection
