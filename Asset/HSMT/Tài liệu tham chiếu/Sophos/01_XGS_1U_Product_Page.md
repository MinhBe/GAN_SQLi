# Sophos XGS Series — 1U Rackmount (Distributed Edge)

Nguồn: https://www.sophos.com/en-us/products/next-gen-firewall/xgs-1u-distributed-edge-firewalls

---

## Mô tả

Midsize and distributed organizations needing a versatile solution to power and protect their networks. These rackmount firewalls offer excellent performance, diverse high-speed built-in interfaces, and add-on connectivity modules. All models powered by high-speed CPU + dedicated Xstream Flow processor for hardware acceleration.

## Connectivity — XGS 1U Models

### XGS 2100 / 2300
- **Fixed Ethernet**: 8×GE copper, 2×SFP fiber
- **Bypass**: 1 pair
- **Max ports**: 18 (1 Flexi Port slot)
- **MGMT**: 1×RJ45, 1×COM RJ45, 1×Micro-USB
- **USB**: 2×USB 3.0 front, 1×USB 2.0 rear
- **2nd PSU**: Optional external

### XGS 3100 / 3300
- **Fixed Ethernet**: 8×GE copper, 2×SFP fiber, **2×SFP+ 10GE fiber**
- **Bypass**: 1 pair
- **Max ports**: 20 (1 Flexi Port slot)
- **MGMT**: 1×RJ45, 1×COM RJ45, 1×Micro-USB
- **USB**: 2×USB 3.0 front, 1×USB 2.0 rear
- **2nd PSU**: Optional external

### XGS 4300 / 4500
- **Fixed Ethernet**: **4×GE copper, 4×2.5GE copper, 4×SFP+ 10GE fiber**
- **Bypass**: **2 pairs**
- **Max ports**: **28** (2 Flexi Port slots)
- **MGMT**: 1×RJ45, 1×COM RJ45, 1×Micro-USB
- **USB**: 2×USB 3.0 front
- **2nd PSU**: Optional external (4300), **Optional internal** (4500)
- **Dual SSD/RAID**: N/A (4300), **Incl. SW RAID-1** (4500)
- **Sliding rails**: Included (4300, 4500)

## Flexi Port Modules (tùy chọn)

- 8-port GE copper
- 8-port GE SFP fiber
- 4-port 10 GE SFP+ fiber
- 4-port GE copper bypass (2 pairs)
- 4-port GE copper PoE + 4-port GE copper
- 2-port GE fiber (LC) bypass + 4-port GE SFP fiber
- 2-port 10 GE NBASE-T + 2-port 10 GE SFP+

## Product Highlights

- Dual-processor architecture (CPU + Xstream Flow Processor)
- Wide selection of copper and fiber ports + management interfaces
- Fixed LAN bypass ports on every model
- Modular Flexi Port expansion bay(s)
- Second power supply optional for all models
- Optional PoE Flexi Port modules, centrally powered
- Rackmount kit included
